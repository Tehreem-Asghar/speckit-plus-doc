# Specification Phase (businees requirment like client)

## **1. Specification Phase — Simple Explanation**
Specification ka matlab hota hai project ka "WHAT" decide karna — hum kya banana chahte hain, kis audience ke liye, kis result tak pohanchna hai. Yahan "HOW" ka matlab coding steps, tools wagaira hote hain, jo plan phase ma aata hai.

Goal: Vague idea ko clear, measurable, simple specification ma convert karna.

---

## **2. Two-Part Workflow**
Specification bananay ka proper method ye hai:

### **(A) Pre-Spec Conversation**
- Yeh aik informal baat cheet hoti hai.
- Isma AI ko interview nahi karwana — aap khud AI sa sawal pochtay ho.
- Aap AI sa questions pochtay ho jaise:
  - "Audience kaun hai?"
  - "Success kaisa dikhtay ga?"
  - "Main kis cheez par focus karun?"
- Is conversation ka purpose: clarity lana.

### **(B) Formal Specification (/sp.specify)**
- Jab information clear ho jaye to aap **/sp.specify** command lagatay ho.
- Output ek structured complete specification hota hai.

---

## **3. Specification Ka Structure**
Specification 4 sections ma hoti hai:

### **1. Intent (WHAT & WHY)**
- Hum kya bana rahe hain?
- Ye kyu bana rahe hain?
- Audience kaun hai?

### **2. Constraints (Limits)**
- Format kya hoga?
- Length kya hogi?
- Scope kitna hoga?
- Kya allowed nahi hai?

### **3. Success Evals (SMART Criteria)**
SMART ka matlab:
- **S**pecific (clear)
- **M**easurable (gini ja sakay)
- **A**chievable (possible ho)
- **R**elevant (project se related ho)
- **T**ime-bound (time limit ho)

Examples:
- "Paper high-quality ho" → Ghalat (subjective)
- "Paper ma 8 peer-reviewed sources ho" → Theek (measureable)

### **4. Non-Goals (Out of Scope)**
- Kya cheez project ka part nahi hai.
- Is sa scope creep nahi hota.

---

## **4. Key Commands**
Yahan sari important commands simple words ma likh di gayi hain:

### **/sp.explore**
- Pre-specification conversation shuru karna.
- Requirement explore karna.

### **/sp.specify**
- Formal specification generate karna.
- AI aapka structured document banata hai.

### **/sp.clarify**
- Specification ko improve karna ka liya use hoti hai ya command Specification ma agar koi doubt ya gap ho, to ye command un gaps ko detect kar kar clarify karti hai.

### **/sp.plan**
- Specification ki base par HOW (steps) bananay ka plan.
- Yeh planning phase hai.

### **/sp.task**
- Plan ko chhotay chhotay tasks ma convert karta hai.
- Har task clear aur doable hota hai.

---

## **5. Common Mistakes (Avoid These)**
- Specification ma "HOW" likhna → Galat.
- Success criteria vague rakhna → Galat.
- Non-goals skip karna → Galat.
- Pre-spec conversation skip karna → Galat.

---

## **6. Quick Checklist (Before Finalizing Spec)**
- Intent clear?
- Constraints specific?
- Success criteria SMART?
- Non-goals likhay?
- Koi HOW leak to nahi hua?
- Kya koi aur person iss spec sa build kar sakta hai?
