# Implement Phase

## 1. Implement Phase Kya Hai?
Implement phase wo hota hai jahan aap **plan aur tasks** ko follow karte hue AI se kaam karwati hain.
Aap direction deti hain, AI kaam karta hai, phir aap check karti hain.

---

## 2. /sp.implement Command
**Command:** `/sp.implement`
- Tasks.md ko read karta hai
- Tasks ko order me run karta hai
- Har task ke baad ruk kar review mangta hai
- Aap approve karti hain to next task chalta hai

---

## 3. Checkpoints
Har kuch tasks complete hone ke baad **checkpoint** aata hai.
Aap ek simple sawal karti hain:
**"Kya ye output spec ke hisab se sahi hai?"**

---

## 4. Calculator App Example
**Spec:** 100–150 words intro, simple language.

AI ne 180 words likh diye.
Aap: "Words zyada hain, 150 tak lao."
AI revise karta hai.
Aap check kar ke approve karti hain.

---

## 5. Jab Output Spec Meet Na Kare
- Words kam hon → "Thora detail add karo"
- Tone wrong ho → "Simple language me likho"
- Features missing hon → "Basic features include karo"

---

## 6. Roles
**Aapka Role:** Direction dena, review karna, approve ya revise karwana

**AI ka Role:** Execute karna, suggest karna, revise karna

---

## 7. Validation Checklist
Har task ke liye check karein:
- Success criteria match?
- Spec follow kiya?
- Quality theek?

---

## 8. Common Mistakes
❌ Review skip kar dena
❌ "Thik lag raha hai" keh kar ignore kar dena
❌ Pehli try me perfect expect karna

---

## 9. Quick Practice Prompts
- "Checkpoint me kya check karna hota hai?"
- "Agar AI ne words zyada likh diye to kya bolna chahiye?"
- "Spec match na kare to revise kaise karwana hai?"
